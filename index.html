Human Foosball

Description :

Human Foosball is like the classic table top Foosball, however, it is played with real people, a soccer ball and in a large enclosed arena. Two teams compete against each other to score the most goals in the allotted time.

Rules :

Players must keep both hands on their respective handles at all times, and one foot must be touching the ground. Shoulders must be kept above the rope at all times. kept above the rope at all times. A goal will be awarded to the shooting team, if the opposing team deliberately takes their hands off the rope to prevent a shot on net. When a goal is scored, the game is restarted by giving possession of the ball to the scored on team in the mid-field area. When the ball goes out of bounds (sides or ends), the game is restarted by giving possession of the ball to the non-offending team in the mid-field area. The game is over when the time expires.

Daastan-e-TV

Description :

Every time we hear the word "Quiz" the only thing matches with it in our mind is "BORING". But this time it's NOT. So here is the event "DAASTAN-E-TV" where you would be asked questions on "TV SERIES". So if you're TV series enthusiasts and if you have whole year Netflix subscription then this is your stage.

Number of participants :

​ 4 Members per team.

The Competition will have Two Rounds: First round will be an Elimination Round in which all the teams will be provided with a sheet having 30 MCQ type questions. Top 5 teams scoring maximum marks will be selected for the ‘Quiz round’. The Quiz Round will have the following sub-rounds - Buzzer Round Questions based on Categories Rapid Fire Round Rules :

Use of internet,mobile phones,notepads,etc.will be strictly prohibited. Decision of the event management team will be final and should be followed by the participants. Any obscenity,vulgarity,profanity of any form will lead to direct disqualification.

Judging Criteria :

The Entire event will be a ‘Points Based Event’. For 1st round, each question will carry equal weightage. There will be negative marking as well for every incorrect answer. In Case of tie in 1st Round, the team which took lesser time to solve the questions will get qualified for the next round. Points distribution for Round Two will be informed to the teams before the start of each sub-round. The team having maximum points at the end of Round Two will be the winner.
